Overview

Purpose

The goal of rafting for our future guests is to experience a thrill-packed outdoor adventure. Rafting offers the opportunity to challenge the rapids while enjoying magnificent landscapes. Whether you are looking for adrenaline or a relaxing getaway, our goal is to provide you with a safe and memorable experience, where you can connect with nature and create unique memories with loved ones or new friends

Audience

Our target audience is adults ages 18-45, of all levels of rafting experience, from diverse professional and geographic backgrounds. They have a common interest in the outdoors and seek unique leisure experiences.
What do they want?They want to experience exciting rafting adventures, connect with nature, create lasting memories and share unforgettable moments with their loved ones. They are looking for varied routes, suitable difficulty levels and an overall exceptional rafting experience.
How will my audience usually access my site?They typically access our site via mobile devices and laptops, searching for information on their smartphones and making more detailed reservations on their desktop computers.
What are their needs that aren't being met?They are looking for reliable information on rafting, advice for preparing their trip, and a user-friendly platform to easily book. They also want access to visual media to better understand the experience.
